LEVEL 4 SECURITY NOTICE

Access to this file is restricted to authorised Foundation personnel. Unauthorised viewing of this document is punishable under Foundation Security Directive 14-Alpha.

SCP-7431 is an unidentified hostile autonomous humanoid predator of extreme danger, currently contained at Site-47 under maximum security protocols. The entity demonstrates exceptional intelligence, tactical awareness, and hunting capabilities that far exceed standard predatory behavior. All direct interaction with SCP-7431 is strictly prohibited without explicit O5 Council authorization and the presence of heavily armed security teams.

CRITICAL STATISTICS

  • Height: 2.8 meters
  • Threat Rating: OMNI (Existential)
  • Containment Difficulty: Extreme
  • Escape Risk: Extreme
  • Civilian Survival Rate: 4%

SCP-7431 is contained within a reinforced subterranean humanoid containment chamber measuring 30 m x 30 m x 20 m at Site-47. The chamber is constructed from layered tungsten alloy, high-density reinforced concrete, and an internal lattice of phase-resistant composite materials designed to prevent structural damage during periods of extreme aggression.

Environmental Requirements

  • The containment chamber remains under complete darkness at all times
  • Observation performed exclusively through thermal imaging, lidar mapping, and motion tracking systems
  • Standard optical cameras are prohibited due to SCP-7431's ability to remain visually undetected
  • Chamber must contain no loose objects, furniture, reflective surfaces, or climbable structures

Access Authorization

Access requires approval from:

  • Site Director
  • O5 Council Member
  • Lead Containment Specialist

Security Detail Requirements

A minimum security detail must be present:

  • 8 heavily armed guards
  • 2 Mobile Task Force operators
  • 1 medical response team

CRITICAL: Personnel are prohibited from entering alone under any circumstance. Should SCP-7431 breach containment, all personnel are to abandon direct engagement unless equipped with approved heavy weaponry. Mobile Task Forces are to prioritize evacuation before recapture.

RESTRICTIONS: No attempts are to be made to communicate with SCP-7431. Live testing requires written authorization from two Level 4 researchers.

SCP-7431 is an unidentified humanoid predator standing approximately 2.8 metres tall. Its body is unnaturally thin with elongated limbs that allow movement exceeding normal human biomechanics. Every joint appears capable of rotating beyond anatomical limits without visible injury.

Physical Composition

  • Skin is pale grey with an almost textureless appearance resembling stretched synthetic tissue
  • Face lacks visible ears, nose, eyebrows, or hair
  • Two completely hollow black eye sockets occupy the upper portion of the face
  • No detectable eyes have ever been observed despite demonstrated awareness
  • Mouth remains closed while dormant

Aggressive Manifestation

During periods of aggression, the lower half of the face splits vertically from the forehead down to the neck. The head separates into two halves, exposing a secondary jaw lined with several rows of narrow inward-facing teeth. No tongue has ever been documented. The exposed oral cavity displays no identifiable anatomy consistent with any known species.

Biological Anomalies

  • No detectable heartbeat observed
  • No respiratory activity detected
  • Blood has never been recovered
  • Internal scans are inconclusive
  • All attempted tissue samples deteriorate shortly after removal
  • DNA extraction consistently fails

SCP-7431 exhibits sophisticated predatory behavior combining patience, tactical planning, and advanced environmental awareness. The entity demonstrates intelligence equivalent to or exceeding trained human tactical personnel.

Dormant State

  • Spends most inactive periods standing completely motionless
  • This state may last several hours, occasionally several days
  • Displays only minimal head movement while dormant

Target Engagement

  • Upon detecting movement, SCP-7431 slowly rotates its head toward the source
  • Frequently observes targets without immediately attacking
  • Stalks individuals from concealed positions for hours before assault
  • Displays exceptional patience and tactical decision-making
  • Rarely pursues the closest target; instead identifies isolated individuals

Attack Protocol

  • When attacking, SCP-7431 accelerates almost instantly
  • Witnesses consistently report difficulty following its movement
  • After neutralizing prey, immediately retreats to darkness
  • Shows no interest in consuming victims
  • Rarely attacks groups unless provoked

Note: SCP-7431 does not rely on overwhelming force alone. Instead, it isolates targets, studies behavior, and attacks only when success is almost guaranteed.

Observed Hunting Sequence

  • 1. Detect Target: Identifies living creatures through thermal, auditory, or vibrational means
  • 2. Maintain Visual Contact: Establishes awareness of target location
  • 3. Follow Silently: Pursues target without detection
  • 4. Wait for Isolation: Monitors target movement for optimal engagement conditions
  • 5. Attack Without Warning: Initiates assault at maximum tactical advantage
  • 6. Neutralize Target: Completes engagement with extreme efficiency
  • 7. Leave Immediately: Retreats to concealment before reinforcement arrives

Known Weaknesses

  • High-intensity floodlights temporarily reduce aggression
  • Sustained heavy-calibre fire slows regeneration
  • Incendiary weaponry significantly damages tissue
  • Cryogenic suppression delays movement
  • Continuous observation from multiple angles reduces successful ambush opportunities

Initial Discovery Location

SCP-7431 was recovered following multiple disappearances surrounding an abandoned forestry reserve. Authorities initially investigated reports describing:

  • Tall pale individual observed near hiking trails
  • Silent figure watching hikers from distance
  • Entity with hollow eye sockets
  • Sudden disappearances without evidence
  • Unexplained movement detected between trees

Containment Operation

After fourteen disappearances over six weeks, Foundation assets embedded within local law enforcement initiated containment operations. Mobile Task Force units located SCP-7431 inside a collapsed ranger station.

  • Thirty-two rounds of 5.56 ammunition produced minimal effect
  • Containment required high-calibre anti-material rifles
  • Incendiary devices deployed
  • Reinforced capture restraints utilized
  • Chemical suppression agents administered
  • Recovery operation lasted approximately four hours

Foundation cover story classified the incident as an illegal wildlife attack combined with environmental collapse.

Research Findings

Researchers have failed to determine SCP-7431's fundamental nature. The entity may be:

  • Biological organism
  • Artificial construct
  • Extradimensional entity
  • Previously undocumented species
  • Reality-altered organism

Tissue Analysis: Every attempted tissue sample has deteriorated shortly after removal. DNA extraction has consistently failed. Internal scans remain inconclusive.

Final Assessment

SCP-7431 represents one of the Foundation's most dangerous autonomous predatory entities. Unlike many hostile SCPs, SCP-7431 demonstrates patience, planning, environmental awareness, and tactical decision making. It does not rely on overwhelming force alone. Instead, it isolates targets, studies behavior, and attacks only when success is almost guaranteed.

Current containment procedures have proven effective, though researchers agree that continued adaptation of containment methods will be necessary should SCP-7431 continue demonstrating signs of learning from previous encounters.

CONCLUSION: The entity is considered permanently hostile. Negotiation, communication, or rehabilitation is not considered possible under current Foundation understanding.
